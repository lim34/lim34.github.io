Why Exercise?


Working out should be a daily routine and part of your daily life! However, you can't start working out until you have some motivation. Why should you work out?


"Exercise helps reduce your blood pressure, in part, by attacking the plaque in your arteries. As the arteries widen, the blood flows through more freely, and your blood pressure eventually starts to drop. Hypertension also decreases as the result of exercise because your heart, a muscle, is getting a workout." -Susan Krauss Whitbourne, Ph.D.
